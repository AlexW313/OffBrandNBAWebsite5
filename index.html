The NBA (National Basketball Association) is headquartered in New York City, with its main offices located in Manhattan. The league, established in 1946, is composed of 30 teams and is one of the major professional sports leagues in North America. As of now, the commissioner of the NBA is Adam Silver, who has held the position since February 2014. Under his leadership, the league has focused on global expansion, social justice initiatives, and enhancing the overall fan experience.
